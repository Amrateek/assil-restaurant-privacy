Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Assil Restaurant ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our WhatsApp ordering service ("the Service").

Information We Collect

When you message our WhatsApp business number to place an order, we collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your name, if provided
  • Your delivery address, if provided for an order
  • Order details (items selected, quantities, payment method)
  • Payment confirmation screenshots, if you choose to pay via InstaPay

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and fulfill your food orders
  • Communicate with you about your order status
  • Confirm payment for orders paid via InstaPay
  • Improve our ordering service

Data Storage and Security

Your order information is stored securely on our servers and is not shared with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your order (e.g. delivery personnel). We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

Data Retention

We retain order information for as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through WhatsApp.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.

Third-Party Services

Our Service uses Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform to send and receive messages. Your use of WhatsApp is also subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our WhatsApp business number.
